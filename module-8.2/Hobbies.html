What are your hobbies?

I also enjoy cooking, so I spend most of my time on the weekends making food for the week or trying to make a new cuisine that I found on YouTube. Aside cooking, binge watching TV shows is another thing that I love doing. After the day is over and there is nothing else to take care of, I get soaked into my Netflix mode and binge on shows that I have heard about or have an interesting preview on Netflix.
